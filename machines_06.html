Model PP 06

Polishing machine with automatic changing tools

About

Model PP06

Fully automatic, allows the placement of several plates for polishing on the table in betao. The plates may have different dimensions, both in length and width and also in height.This is possible because after the programming of the device, it starts operating at the desired cycle.

After the polishing of a plate automatically goes to the following, fulfilling the cycle of pre programmed measures. If not available from automatic exchange of polishing heads, ended a cutting cycle, the head is positioned next to the Framework electric, the same happens when decriptions station too shabby, with both an audible signal to the operator.

This can program and exchange material without stopping the machine, thus having a completely full production without interruptions. The axes is done through Linear Guides, allowing high speeds with great precision. Lubrication of fully automatic displacement guides.
