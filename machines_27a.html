About

Machine 27a

This machine allows the automatic slab cutting, development with base on our several bridge saw, maintains the same type of displacement systems, same programation, using an combination of various technics and systems of the our fast and very quality machines we create this fast and simple slab cutting machine. An very automatic machine at a very low cost.

Cutting capacity : All types of stone, since marbles, limestones, granites or basalt and non natural stone... The cutting travel allows an slab with 130 inch's or 137,5 inch's (depends of the disk guard assembled) by 70,5 inch's (3,5meters x 1,8meters), working with 23,5 inch's disk diameter can cut 7 inch's of thickness.

Advantages : Independent table built in steel frame, it allows the operator circulate all around and remove the stone all ready done without move the complete slab, allow to proceed another automatic programation in the same material. The concept of steel frame allow to reduce the assembling time, transport space, less manufacture time, it can be done thanks to direct power transmission of 15 hp engine and an single body bridge.

Programation capacity : 20 measures with each 99 quantities , disk thickness on proper cell , definition of limit depth of cut , memorisation where the slab is to make de automatic cycle.

System of working for all models with console programation: With an automatic work system by a simple programation it allows the operator no needs too much experience to execute the cutting orders on the equipment. The principle it's simple, after charge the machine with an or several stone slabs, with an hand console he moves the disk and with the lazer alignment can positioning on the lateral of stone to start the job, memorise that point and the machine receive the initial cutting coordinates, program in board console the measures and also quantities, check the parameters like height of each cutting moviment, than pressing the start button to run the main engine after check if everything ok the water automatic runs on disk guard and are able to press the start automatic operation button, on first displacement movement he regulate the cutting velocity, the length of the slab and the machine do the rest, with several alarm and protection systems it prepared to work all the automatic cycle without the man intervention.

Occupation space : In transportation;

On working area Energy necessary : Cable 3x plus earth condutor;

Water consume : Connection at ;

Base components : Automatic lubrification system to every movement part's, lazer to align the first cutting, pneumatic valve to stop water after automatic cycle, automatic detection of low water pressure, pine wood coated table, electrical engines in displacements, Controlled displacement velocity by inverter's as so the disk rotation, head rotation in hydraulic system, electrical board with simple access, protection guard to lateral mud projection, steel surface treated with zinc layer, water hose in special form like used in high pressure air circuit, electrical cables with flexible allow and internal metallic net in coating. As extras only the hydraulic table to charge the slab, all the rest are in standard.
